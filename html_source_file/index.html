About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Andlinger Center for Energy and the Environment (ACEE), Princeton University[cite: 2]. Currently, I am also a Schmidt Sciences Visiting Research Scholar at IIT Delhi. My research leads net-zero India modelling and downscaling efforts, focusing on geo-spatial modelling for decarbonization[cite: 17, 22].

Research Interests

  • Energy Systems: Decarbonization pathways, net-zero modelling, and VRE siting[cite: 18, 24].
  • GIS & Remote Sensing: Renewable energy potential assessment and decentralized energy planning[cite: 37, 85].
